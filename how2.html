How to Play

Game Setup

  • Players: 3–6
  • Time: 5–10 minutes

Turn Mechanics

  • Draws a card from the deck
  • Plays one card to sabotage, defend, or take action

Fraud Investigations

  • If a player is hit with a "Fraud Investigation" and cannot block it, they are out of the game

Winning the Game

  • The last player remaining wins
  • If no one is eliminated by the end of the deck, the player with the fewest cards left wins
